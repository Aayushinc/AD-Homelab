Lab 3: Add UPN suffix in on-prem Windows AD

This part is on your Windows Server DC.

A. Add a new UPN suffix
1. On your Domain Controller, open Server Manager.
2. Click Tools > Active Directory Domains and Trusts.
3. In the left pane, right-click Active Directory Domains and Trusts at the top.
4. Click Properties.
5. Under Alternative UPN suffixes, enter your custom suffix, such as:
- org.com
6. Click Add.
7. Click OK.

B. Apply the new UPN to a user
1. Open Active Directory Users and Computers.
2. Open the OU where your test user exists.
3. Right-click the user and open Properties.
4. Go to the Account tab.
5. In the user logon name area, change the suffix from lab.local to org.com.
6. Click Apply and OK.
