Lab 1: M365 tenant creation
A. Create the tenant
1. Sign in to the Microsoft Entra admin center.
2. Go to Entra ID.
3. Open Overview.
4. Click Manage tenants.
5. Click Create.
6. Choose Governed Workforce.
7. On the next screen, enter:
- Organization name
- Initial domain name such as yourlaborg.onmicrosoft.com
- Country/Region
8. Review and create the tenant.
9. After creation, switch into the new tenant from Manage tenants.

B. Confirm the tenant is ready
1. In the Entra admin center, go to Entra ID > Overview.
2. Note:
- Tenant name
- Tenant ID
3. Primary domain
