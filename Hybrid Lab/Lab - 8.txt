Lab 8: Outlook / Exchange Online distribution groups for Sales, IT, HR, Ops

What you want here is a distribution group for each department so mail sent to sales@org.com, it@org.com, and so on reaches all users in that department. Microsoft supports this through the Exchange admin center and also the Microsoft 365 admin center.

A. Create the distribution groups in Exchange admin center
1. Go to the Exchange admin center.
2. Open Recipients > Groups or Recipients > Groups > Distribution list, depending on the UI version.
3. Click Add a group.
4. Choose Distribution.
5. Enter:
- Group name: Sales
- Email alias/address: sales@org.com
6. Add owners if prompted.
7. Add members from your Sales users.
8. Review and create.
9. Repeat for:
- IT → it@org.com
- HR → hr@org.com
- Ops → ops@org.com

Microsoft’s Exchange documentation shows this flow through Recipients > Groups, where you choose Distribution group or Distribution list in the creation wizard.

B. Test mail flow
1. Open Outlook on the web or another mailbox.
2. Send a test email to sales@org.com.
3. Confirm all Sales group members receive it.
4. Repeat for the other groups.
